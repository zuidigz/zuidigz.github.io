About Me

Hi! My name is Zui and I recently graduated from Duke University with a degree in Biomedical Engineering and Computer Science.

My experiences at school have taught me how to nurture an idea from its nascent stages to a full blown product–whether that be creating an innovative open source device, founding a startup, or managing the technology division of a dynamic student run company. I look forward to the possibility growing products that change how we interact with the world.

I view every challenge as a chance to grow, and am the first person to jump at the opportunity to share what I know with those around me, holding the firm belief that success is best when others grow with you. As a self-starter, I hope to bring these same open ideals to the world and ensure that everyone, regardless of their background or status, is able to have the same resources to create.
